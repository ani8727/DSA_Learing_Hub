# Two Pointer Pattern

## Overview
- The Two Pointer technique uses two pointers to solve problems efficiently.
- Commonly used for searching pairs, subarrays, or linked list problems.

## Tricks & Patterns
- Moving pointers inward/outward.
- Fast and slow pointers for cycle detection.
- Sliding window for subarray problems.
